Frequently asked

Is there really an AI that speaks Zulu and Afrikaans? ▼
Yes. Naledi detects which language a customer writes in and responds in the same language — English, isiZulu, or Afrikaans. Code-switching mid-conversation is handled naturally.
Do customers need to download an app? ▼
No. It works on normal WhatsApp. Customers message your existing business number and Naledi replies instantly. Nothing to install.
What happens when I reach my daily limit? ▼
Naledi politely tells the customer you will get back to them soon and forwards the enquiry to your phone. You never miss a lead.
Can I upgrade or change plans later? ▼
Yes. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel anytime. Changes take effect immediately. No lock-in contracts.
