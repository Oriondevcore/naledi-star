Privacy Policy

Last updated: 22 June 2026

POPIA Compliant | Protection of Personal Information Act, 2013

1. Who We Are

We are Orion Ventures, operated by Graham Henry. We provide the Naledi AI WhatsApp receptionist service. Graham Henry is the Information Officer responsible for POPIA compliance.

2. What Information We Collect

From customers (people who message a business using Naledi):

  • WhatsApp phone number
  • Messages you send (text, voice notes, images, documents)
  • Name (if you choose to share it)
  • Any other information you voluntarily provide during the conversation

From business clients:

  • Your name and WhatsApp number
  • Your business name, address, and details
  • Your business's WhatsApp number to connect with Naledi
  • Knowledge base content you upload (price lists, menus, FAQs, documents)
  • Billing information (payment is processed by a third-party provider — we never see your full card details)

3. How We Collect Information

  • Directly from you when you message Naledi on WhatsApp
  • From business clients when they set up their account and upload documents
  • Automatically through the WhatsApp platform (message metadata)

4. Purpose of Processing

We process your information to:

  • Answer your messages (the core function of Naledi)
  • Capture leads and enquiries for the business you contacted
  • Improve Naledi's responses and accuracy
  • Send invoices, service updates, and support messages
  • Comply with legal obligations (POPIA, tax records)

5. Legal Basis for Processing (POPIA Section 11)

  • Consent — you choose to message Naledi and share your information
  • Contractual necessity — processing is necessary to provide the service
  • Legitimate interest — improving the service and protecting our legal rights
  • Legal obligation — retaining records as required by South African law

6. Who We Share Information With

We use these third-party service providers (all bound by data processing agreements):

  • Cloudflare — hosting, database, AI processing (global edge network)
  • Z.AI (Zhipu AI) — AI model for message understanding (China — message text processed but not stored)
  • WhatsApp / Meta — messaging platform (message transit only)
  • Yoco / PayFast (future) — payment processing (payment amount only, no card details)

We never: sell your personal information, use conversations to train other AI models, share data with advertisers, or process data beyond what's described here.

7. Cross-Border Data Transfer (POPIA Section 72)

Message understanding is processed through Z.AI's API in China. This is a restricted transfer under POPIA. We ensure:

  • Z.AI processes data only for generating responses
  • Z.AI does not store or retain message content
  • Messages are transmitted securely via HTTPS
  • You consent to this transfer by using the service

8. Data Retention

  • Conversations: retained while account is active, deleted within 60 days of cancellation
  • Business knowledge base: retained while account is active, deleted within 60 days of cancellation
  • Financial records: retained for 5 years as required by South African tax law
  • Chat logs (anonymized): may be retained longer for service improvement (de-identified)

9. Data Security

  • Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Encryption at rest (Cloudflare infrastructure)
  • Access controls — only Graham has access to the system
  • Regular security reviews
  • Cloudflare's enterprise-grade infrastructure

10. Your Rights Under POPIA

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of your personal information we hold
  • Correction — request that we correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion — request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Objection — object to the processing of your personal information
  • Restriction — request restriction of processing
  • Portability — request a copy of your data in a structured format
  • Complain — lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator

To exercise any of these rights, contact Graham on WhatsApp at +27 72 497 1810.
