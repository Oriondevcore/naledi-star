3. Payment

Monthly in advance, charged on the same day each month. If payment fails for 7 consecutive days, service will be paused until payment is received. We will notify you before pausing.

4. Client Responsibilities

  • Provide accurate business information for Naledi's knowledge base
  • Inform your customers they are speaking to an AI
  • Not use Naledi for illegal, fraudulent, or harmful purposes
  • Regularly review conversations to ensure quality
  • Maintain accurate knowledge base content

5. Our Commitments

  • Naledi will be available 24/7 (excluding planned maintenance)
  • Your data is yours — we never sell it
  • Conversations are private and secure
  • We will fix errors as quickly as possible
  • We will notify you of any material changes to the service

6. AI Acknowledgment

You acknowledge that Naledi uses artificial intelligence and may make mistakes. Critical information should be verified. We continuously improve but cannot guarantee 100% accuracy.

By us: 30 days notice if we discontinue the service.

For breach: Immediate termination if you violate these terms (illegal use, abuse, fraud).

9. Data Protection

Your data is processed in accordance with our Privacy Policy and POPIA. You are the data controller for your customers' information; we act as the data processor.

10. Governing Law

This agreement is governed by South African law. Disputes will first go to mediation before court proceedings.
