2. Records Available Without Request

The following records are automatically available:

  • Our Privacy Policy (on our website and via WhatsApp)
  • Our Terms of Service (on our website)
  • Our Service Agreement (provided to clients)
  • Pricing information (provided on request)

3. Description of Records Held

Client records:

  • Client name, contact details, billing information
  • Service agreements and communications
  • Knowledge base content uploaded by clients
  • Conversation logs (WhatsApp messages with Naledi)
  • Usage statistics and analytics

Operational records:

  • Internal communications
  • Financial records and invoices
  • Service improvement data (anonymized)
  • Security logs and access records

4. Subject Categories of Records

Records are held relating to: clients (businesses using Naledi), end users (customers who message Naledi), service providers (Cloudflare, Z.AI, WhatsApp), and financial transactions.

5. Request Procedure

Requests for access to records must:

  • Be made in writing (WhatsApp or email)
  • Provide sufficient detail to identify the record
  • Specify the preferred format (electronic or paper)
  • Include proof of identity

6. Fees

The prescribed fees for access are:

  • Request fee: R50 (standard application fee)
  • Access fee: R1.00 per page (or R5.00 per electronic file)
  • Actual cost for preparation time
