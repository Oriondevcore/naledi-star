Frequently Asked Questions

Last updated: 22 June 2026

What is Naledi?

Naledi is an AI receptionist that answers your business WhatsApp messages 24/7. She qualifies leads, answers questions, books appointments, and transcribes voice notes. She speaks English, Zulu, and Afrikaans.

Do my customers know they're talking to AI?

Yes. Naledi identifies herself as an AI assistant. We require all clients to be transparent with their customers.

Do I need a website?

No. Naledi works entirely through WhatsApp. We can build you a website if you want one, but it's not required to get started.

What happens if Naledi doesn't know the answer?

She politely tells the customer she'll pass them to you, and you get a WhatsApp notification with their details.

Can I review conversations?

Yes. You get access to the business dashboard where you can see all messages, leads captured, and conversation history.

How fast is the setup?

Most clients are live within 48 hours. We train Naledi on your business, connect your WhatsApp number, and test everything before going live.

What if I don't want Naledi to reply to something?

You control the knowledge base. If you don't teach her about a specific topic, she won't answer. She always defers pricing and booking confirmations to you.

Is my data safe?

Yes. All conversations are encrypted. Data is stored on Cloudflare's infrastructure (not Meta's). We never sell your data or use it to train other companies' AI.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. 7 days written notice. No penalties. We delete your data within 60 days of cancellation.

What happens if WhatsApp goes down?

Naledi pauses until WhatsApp is back. We can't control WhatsApp's uptime, but we notify you if there's an extended outage.

How much does it cost?

Pricing is being finalised. Contact us on WhatsApp for current pricing. We offer premium AI receptionist services — not cheap chatbots.

What payment methods do you accept?

Card payments (coming soon via Yoco). Manual EFT available for now. We'll invoice you.
