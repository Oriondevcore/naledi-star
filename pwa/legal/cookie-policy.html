Cookie Policy

Last updated: 22 June 2026

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the website work properly and provide information to the site owner.

Cookies We Use

We use minimal cookies on our business dashboards and website:

CookiePurposeDurationType
session_idKeep you logged into the dashboardSessionEssential

Third-Party Cookies

We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or analytics cookies. Cloudflare (our hosting provider) may set essential security cookies to protect against DDoS attacks.

Managing Cookies

You can disable cookies in your browser settings. However, essential cookies are required for the dashboard and PWA to function. Disabling them may break these features.

No Tracking

We do not track your browsing behavior across websites. We do not use Google Analytics or similar tracking tools.

Changes

We may update this Cookie Policy. Changes will be posted on our website.
